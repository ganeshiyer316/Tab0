Privacy Policy

Overview

This privacy policy explains how the Tab Age Tracker Web Dashboard handles your data. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your information.

Data Storage

The Tab Age Tracker Web Dashboard is designed to respect your privacy:

  • We do not collect or store any of your browsing data on our servers
  • All tab information is stored locally in your browser through the Extension
  • When you use the Web Dashboard, data is only temporarily loaded in your browser for visualization
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties

How Data Is Transferred to the Web Dashboard

There are two ways to view your tab data in the Web Dashboard:

  1. Direct File Upload: You can export your tab data from the extension as a JSON file and upload it to the web dashboard. This file transfer happens entirely within your browser and is not sent to any servers.
  2. Extension Link: The extension can open the dashboard with your data in the URL. The data is base64 encoded and included in the URL. No data is sent to our servers - it's just a parameter used to pre-populate the dashboard.

Cookies and Tracking

The Tab Age Tracker Web Dashboard:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not track your browsing activity
  • Does not contain any analytics scripts
  • Does not use any third-party services

Data Security

Since all data processing happens locally in your browser and no data is stored on servers, the only security concern is with the data on your own device. We recommend following standard security practices:

  • Keep your browser updated
  • Use a secure password for your device
  • Be cautious when sharing exported data files

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue in our repository.
